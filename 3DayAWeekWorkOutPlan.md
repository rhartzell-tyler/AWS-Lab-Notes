# 3‑Day Weekly Strength Plan  
*Equipment:* 10 lb dumbbells, adjustable bench  
*Format:* Each day is a circuit. Start with 1 round for 2 weeks, then 2 rounds, then 3 as able.  
*Reps:* 8–12 per exercise unless noted  
*Rest:* 60–90 seconds between rounds  

---

## Day 1 — Chest, Shoulders, Triceps, Biceps (Push + Arms)

1. *Dumbbell Bench Press (Flat Bench)*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Slow, controlled lowering

2. *Seated Shoulder Press (Bench Upright)*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Keep core tight, avoid arching

3. *Incline Dumbbell Chest Fly*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Light stretch at bottom, don’t overextend

4. *Dumbbell Lateral Raises*  
   - 10–12 reps  
   - Soft elbows, raise to shoulder height

5. *Tricep Overhead Extension (One or Two Dumbbells)*  
   - 10–12 reps  
   - Keep elbows close to head

6. *Dumbbell Bicep Curls*  
   - 10–12 reps  
   - Full range, no swinging

---

## Day 2 — Legs, Glutes, Core (Lower Body)

1. *Goblet Squat*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Chest up, sit back into hips

2. *Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Hinge at hips, slight knee bend

3. *Dumbbell Step‑Ups (Bench or Sturdy Surface)*  
   - 8–10 reps per leg  
   - Drive through heel

4. *Glute Bridge or Hip Thrust (Bench Optional)*  
   - 12–15 reps  
   - Squeeze glutes at top

5. *Standing Calf Raises*  
   - 12–15 reps  
   - Pause at top

6. *Core: Dead Bug or Plank*  
   - 20–30 seconds

---

## Day 3 — Back, Rear Shoulders, Biceps, Stability (Pull + Balance)

1. *Dumbbell Bent‑Over Row*  
   - 8–12 reps  
   - Keep back flat, pull elbows back

2. *Single‑Arm Dumbbell Row (Bench Support)*  
   - 8–12 reps per arm  
   - Full stretch at bottom

3. *Rear Delt Fly (Bent Over or Incline Bench)*  
   - 10–12 reps  
   - Light weight, slow tempo

4. *Hammer Curls*  
   - 10–12 reps  
   - Neutral grip, controlled lowering

5. *Farmer Carry*  
   - 20–30 seconds  
   - Walk tall, shoulders back

6. *Bird Dog (Core + Stability)*  
   - 6–8 reps per side  
   - Slow and controlled

---

## Progression Plan

- *Weeks 1–2:* 1 circuit per day  
- *Weeks 3–4:* 2 circuits per day  
- *Weeks 5–6:* 2–3 circuits depending on energy  
- Increase dumbbells gradually (10 → 12 → 15 lbs) only when reps feel smooth  
- Keep reps in the 8–12 range  
- Always leave 1–2 reps in the tank  

---

## Notes

- Sessions should take 15–25 minutes  
- Consistency matters more than intensity  
- Focus on slow, controlled reps  
- Stop any movement that causes sharp pain
